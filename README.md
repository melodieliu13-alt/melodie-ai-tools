# I use AI to find better growth opportunities and move on them faster.

I'm Melodie Liu, a user-growth operator with eight years of experience across education, FinTech and Web3.

My job is simple to explain: **find the right people, understand what they need, and turn that relationship into growth.** AI helps me research faster, prepare better and run work that would otherwise need a much larger team.

## Three examples

### 1. I used AI to find and develop new business partners

At OKX, finding new affiliate partners meant searching across local crypto, quantitative-trading and FinTech communities, researching each prospect, understanding what they needed and preparing a relevant offer.

I brought AI into the whole process:

**find prospects → research their content and background → match their needs → prepare onboarding material → compare our offer with competitors**

**Result:** I personally opened **5 new cross-sector partner nodes**. The team opened about **15**; one quantitative-trading node developed with the team reached **200M USDT in monthly trading volume** and became a super node.

### 2. I used AI to help one person run a 1,200-partner campaign

During OKX's 2025 Lunar New Year campaign, the problem was not the campaign idea. The problem was execution: roughly 1,200 partners needed daily tracking, clear instructions and fast follow-up.

I used AI to turn the campaign rules and daily data into simple, partner-level actions—what needed attention today, who should be followed up with and what the team should say.

**Result:** our team ranked **#1 among five teams** on both campaign participation and first-trade rate. First-trade rate reached **16–17%**, compared with **5–6%** for the other teams.

### 3. I built a faster way to decide which KOLs are worth pursuing

KOL research used to mean opening profiles one by one, reading timelines and trying to remember who looked promising. That worked for ten accounts, not hundreds.

I built a Chrome extension and an AI search tool that turned **197 accounts and 11,100 posts** into a searchable research library. Instead of rereading timelines, I can ask a question, compare accounts and sort prospects into four working groups:

- **A:** worth deep research and a tailored proposal;
- **B/C:** relationships to develop and places to test the pitch;
- **D:** not an active target, but still useful for understanding the market.

## How I work with AI

I decide what problem is worth solving, what a useful answer should look like and whether the result is good enough to use. AI helps me build the tool, process the repetitive work and iterate quickly.

Across my growth career, I managed roughly **300 affiliate partners** and produced more than **25M USDT in cumulative profit** at OKX; moved first trades from **26 to 144** and rebates up **65%** in one KOL case; and reached an **18% expansion rate** at Zuoyebang against a 13–14% industry benchmark.
