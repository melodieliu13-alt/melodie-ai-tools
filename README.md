# I turn fragmented partner signals into growth decisions.

I'm Melodie Liu, a global growth operator with eight years of experience across education, FinTech and Web3. At OKX, I managed a portfolio of roughly 300 affiliate partners that generated 25M+ USDT in cumulative profit.

I use AI when research or execution stops scaling. AI helps me find and process evidence; I decide which opportunity to pursue, what action to take and when the evidence is not strong enough.

## Start here — KOL Partner Intelligence

KOL research used to mean opening profiles one by one, reading timelines and trying to remember who looked promising. That worked for ten accounts, not hundreds.

I built a Chrome extension and an MCP search tool that made **197 accounts and 11,100 posts** queryable. The workflow turns scattered public material into evidence a growth operator can inspect, compare and use in a partner decision.

**Before → After:** repeated timeline reading → searchable evidence → source comparison → a bounded recommendation and next action.

## What I decide — and where AI helps

**I own:** problem choice, business logic, partner fit, acceptance standards, interpretation, privacy and the final decision.
